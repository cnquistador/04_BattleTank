CONTROLS

Keyboard:
W = Forward
A = Turn Left
S = Reverse
D = Turn Right

Rotating Turret/Elevating Barrel = Mouse

Controller:
Left Trigger = Forward Left Track
Right Trigger = Forward Right Track
Both Triggers = Forward

Left Bumper = Reverse Left Track
Right Bumper = Reverse Right Track
Both Bumpers = Reverse

Rotating Turret/Elevating Barrel = Right Stick
